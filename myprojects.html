🚀 Strategic Transformation Dashboard (PowerBI)

📌 Purpose

To support the company’s transformation programme, I developed a Power BI dashboard that provides stakeholders and senior management with a consolidated, real-time view of all ongoing strategic initiatives.

🔍 What I Did

  • Took the initiative to move away from slow, manual Excel reports and build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
  • Consolidated data from various sources including finance systems, Excel trackers, and even email submissions from initiative owners.
  • Calculated high-level ROI for each initiative based on projected cost savings and revenue benefits.
  • Merged actual vs. budgeted investment data with benefit realization tracking to give a holistic view of performance.
  • Connected additional metadata such as initiative status (RAG), business pillars, and ownership to enhance visibility and filtering.
  • Dealt with data inconsistencies and gaps by standardizing inputs and collaborating with business units to clean and validate information.

🎯 Outcome & Business Impact

  • Empowered the Transformation Office & Leadership to monitor Monitor initiatives at a glance across business pillars
  • Enabled stakeholders to self-serve and validate their own initiative data.
  • Supported executive decision-making on prioritization, investment, and next steps.

🛠️ Tools Used

Power BI, Excel, stakeholder interviews.
